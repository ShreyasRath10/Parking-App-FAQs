Parking App FAQs

Q1) How does the app determine the proximity of available parking spots to the user's destination?

Answer: The app utilizes a combination of sophisticated distance and time algorithms to precisely determine the proximity of available parking spots to the user's destination, ensuring an optimal and efficient parking experience.

Q2) Are there any limitations to the accuracy of parking spot detection using the AI synthesizer?

Answer: The accuracy of parking spot detection may be influenced by factors such as camera quality and placement. In challenging conditions, occasional limitations may arise, but the AI synthesizer will evolve over time and apply advanced algorithms for robust and reliable performance.

Q3) How is the revenue generated from ads in the Simple Version distributed among sponsors and footage providers?

Answer: The revenue generated from ads in the Simple Version is distributed among sponsors and footage providers, creating a symbiotic relationship where commercial shops benefit from advertising revenue, incentivizing them to provide surveillance footage for enhanced parking detection.
